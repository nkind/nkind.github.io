2 Steps Back

a mobile GPS adventure game

Created for the fourth-year gaming workshop course at the University of Saskatchewan. The course followed an Agile Scrum style workflow with two-week sprints. As part of a team of 12 students, I was in the designer and product manager role due to the concept of the game stemming from my initial idea.

This was a highly collaborative project that granted me a lot of valuable work experience in effective communication, listening, and the ability to deliver on firm deadlines. I took on a leadership position as the game follows a 50-page script that I wrote individually then worked together with the rest of the team to create
