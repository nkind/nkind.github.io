Team Jamm'n

a four-player online cooperative game

A group of five students that created a fully functional online multiplayer game in three-months as part of the game design course at the University of Saskatchewan using Unity. It was a cooperative experience focusing on working together to progress through levels filled with enemies and traps to get to the final boss.

Each player is in control of an element that can be combined with another player's to create enhanced attacks. Still, if they aren't careful and mix with an element that is not complimentary, an adverse effect is also possible.
